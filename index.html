Meet Andy

I ran a theater for 15 years before I ever coached an executive.

That's not a quirky backstory, it's the reason the work actually works. When you direct live theater at a high level, you learn to read a room the way most people read a spreadsheet. You learn that talented people don't automatically become a great ensemble, that trust isn't a soft skill but the infrastructure everything else runs on, and that the conversation nobody wants to have is almost always the one that changes everything.

I brought all of that into executive team coaching because I kept watching leadership teams make the same mistake: investing in strategy while ignoring the fact that the people at the top couldn't be honest with each other. I work with CEOs and their teams to fix that, and I work with individual leaders who are ready to stop performing confidence and start actually leading.

Who We Work With

This work isn't for everyone.

It's for leaders who already know something needs to change. We partner with executive teams and senior leaders at privately held businesses and nonprofits, typically between 20 and 500 employees, in healthcare, professional services, tech, construction, and nonprofits serving children. We keep our client roster intentionally small because this work requires real attention, not a revolving door of engagements where nobody remembers your name.

What We Do

How we work with leaders and their teams.

Executive Team Coaching

Most leadership teams are a collection of strong individuals who happen to report to the same person, and that's the whole problem. We work with the CEO and their team together through The Six Shifts™ to rebuild how they trust each other, fight productively, share ownership of outcomes, and make decisions that actually stick. This is the engagement that changes the trajectory of a company, not because we bring some magic framework, but because we force the conversations that should have happened two years ago.

Learn more →

1:1 Executive Coaching

Some leaders have a growth edge they can feel but can't quite name, the thing that keeps showing up in every hard conversation, every personnel decision, every moment where the stakes get real. We work one-on-one with senior leaders to surface those patterns, challenge them directly, and build new ways of operating that show up in actual business results. This isn't therapy, and it isn't cheerleading. It's structured, honest partnership with someone who will tell you what your team won't.

Speaking & Facilitation

Every keynote and offsite we run is built from scratch for your team, your context, and the specific challenge you're trying to solve. We don't recycle slide decks or deliver generic leadership content that could apply to any room. If you're bringing your leadership team together, we'll make sure they leave with something that actually shifts how they think and work, not just a good feeling that fades by Monday.
