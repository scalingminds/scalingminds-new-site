Meet Andy

I've spent my career studying what makes teams work.

For 15 years I led creative teams at one of Chicago's premier live theaters. I watched what happened when talented people trusted each other enough to do their best work. And what happened when they didn't.

That experience shaped everything about how I work with executive teams and individual leaders today. Most leadership teams have the talent, the strategy, and the ambition. What they don't have is the trust, the candor, or the shared accountability to execute together.
