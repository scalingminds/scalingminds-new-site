Meet Andy

I've spent my career studying what makes teams work.

For 17 years I led creative teams at one of Chicago's premier live theaters. Night after night, I watched what happened when talented people trusted each other enough to do their best work together. And what happened when they didn't.

That experience shaped everything about how I work with executive teams today. I created The Six Shifts™ because most leadership teams have the talent, the strategy, and the ambition. What they don't have is the trust, the candor, or the shared accountability to execute together.

I work with CEOs and their leadership teams to close that gap. Not with a one-off workshop. With a system that changes how the team operates every day, in every meeting, on every decision.
