Executive Team Coaching

Your leadership team isn't operating like a team.

They're talented individually. But in the room together, real conversations don't happen. Accountability is inconsistent. Decisions get made and then quietly unmade. The CEO ends up carrying the weight because nobody else steps up the same way.

We work with the CEO and their leadership team — together — to reset how the team communicates, makes decisions, and holds each other accountable. The work is built on The Six Shifts™, our proprietary leadership operating system that moves teams from surface-level agreement to real trust, candor, and shared ownership.

This isn't a workshop. It's an ongoing engagement — typically 6 to 12 months — that changes how the team operates in every meeting, every decision, every day.
