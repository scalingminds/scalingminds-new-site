You know something needs to change.
Here's how we help.

Most leaders come to us in one of two situations: the team isn't working and they know it, or they personally feel stuck and need someone in their corner. Sometimes both.

We don't hand you a menu. Every engagement starts with a conversation about what's actually happening — in the business, on the team, and with you as a leader. Then we build something around that. Here are the three ways we typically work with clients.

Executive Team Coaching

Your leadership team isn't operating like a team.

They're talented individually. But in the room together, real conversations don't happen. Accountability is inconsistent. Decisions get made and then quietly unmade. The CEO ends up carrying the weight because nobody else steps up the same way.

We work with the CEO and their leadership team — together — to reset how the team communicates, makes decisions, and holds each other accountable. The work is built on The Six Shifts™, our proprietary leadership operating system that moves teams from surface-level agreement to real trust, candor, and shared ownership.

This isn't a workshop. It's an ongoing engagement — typically 6 to 12 months — that changes how the team operates in every meeting, every decision, every day.

1:1 Executive Coaching

You're carrying more than you should be. And it's starting to show.

Maybe you're a CEO who knows they're the bottleneck but can't figure out how to let go. Maybe you're a VP navigating a political minefield and losing sleep over it. Maybe you're a leader who keeps hitting the same ceiling and the books and podcasts aren't cutting it anymore.

This is direct, structured coaching tied to real business outcomes. We work on the patterns you can't see in yourself — the motivations, the fears, the default reactions that drive every decision you make. When those shift, everything shifts: how you lead, how your team responds, how the business performs.

Engagements are typically 6 to 12 months. We meet regularly, and the work happens between sessions as much as during them.
