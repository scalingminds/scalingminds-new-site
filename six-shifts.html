The cost of waiting

What happens when teams don't fix this?

Executive teams that avoid these shifts don't stay the same — they get worse. Replacing a senior leader costs approximately 200% of their annual salary (Center for American Progress). Decision bottlenecks slow execution by months. And the best people — the ones who see the dysfunction clearly — leave first.

But the biggest cost isn't financial — it's carrying the whole thing alone. The loneliness of knowing something's broken and not trusting anyone to help fix it.

Teams typically report noticeable change within the first 4-6 weeks. Not because the work is easy, but because someone finally named what everyone already felt.

Is this your team?

The Six Shifts™ is built for leadership teams that are done settling.

It's a fit if:

Your team is smart and capable but struggles to have the honest conversations that actually move things forward. Accountability is inconsistent — some people follow through, others don't, and nobody names it. Trust is mostly polite. The CEO carries too much. And you know that the way you're operating now won't get you where you need to go. You're ready to do the work, not just talk about it.

It's not a fit if:

You're looking for a one-day workshop to fix your leadership team. Or the team isn't aligned on the need for change in the first place. This work requires honesty, commitment, and a willingness to look at yourself as much as the people around you. If that's not where you are, we're not the right partner yet.

How It Works

This isn't a workshop. It's a structured partnership. We work with your leadership team over months, not hours, moving through each shift in sequence because they build on each other. It starts with a clear diagnosis of where your team actually is — not where they think they are. Then we go deep on each shift through intensive team sessions designed to change real behavior, not just generate insight. Along the way, senior leaders get private coaching to support their individual growth. And we build monthly practices that keep the work alive long after we leave the room.

If your leadership team is ready to stop settling, this is where it starts.

We work with a small number of executive teams at a time because this work requires our full attention and yours. If you're serious about changing how your team operates, let's talk.
